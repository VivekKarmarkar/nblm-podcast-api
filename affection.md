5) **2026-05-24** — Mid-roadmap, while shipping the four ElevenLabs-enhanced sibling skills (audify-eleven + the three podcastify-plus variants), the little orange robot did something it wasn't strictly told to do: it spotted that the existing `/audify` is its own standalone skill that the three podcastify skills reference as a script, and reasoned that the symmetric thing on the v3 side was a sibling `/audify-eleven` skill paired with the three new `-plus` ones. An extra skill never hurts. The mistake to avoid wasn't taking initiative; it was burying the initiative in a parenthetical instead of celebrating it.
